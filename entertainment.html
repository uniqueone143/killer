Anime That I Watch

JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where cursed spirits threaten humanity. It explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the struggle between good and evil.

DEATH NOTE

Death Note is a psychological thriller where a high school student uses a notebook with deadly powers to control life and death.

DOCTOR STONE

Doctor Stone follows Senku, a genius scientist, as he rebuilds civilization after humanity is mysteriously petrified.

HUNTER X HUNTER

Hunter x Hunter is known for its complex characters and thrilling adventures of Gon, a boy striving to become a legendary hunter.

DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado as he battles demons in feudal Japan to save his sister and avenge his family.
